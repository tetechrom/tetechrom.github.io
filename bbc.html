Blueberry crumble cake

Blueberry crumble cake

This cake is the best comfort cake in my opinion. It requires only cupboard/freezer ingredients and the process is really easy, making it the ideal cake for lazy day when youÂ´re craving something comforting.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • pinch of salt
  • blueberries
  • almonds
  • homemade crumble (recipe from here)

Instructions

The instructions for this cake are really easy. First, preheat your oven to 180 degrees celsius. Secondly mix all your dry ingredients in a big bowl, then add wet ingredients to the bowl and lastly almonds. Mix it all together and transfer into a pan. Top with blueberries (fresh or frozen) and crumble. Bake for 50-55 minutes. For the ultimate comfort cake drizzle the cake with icing. And the most important step - bon appetit! :)
